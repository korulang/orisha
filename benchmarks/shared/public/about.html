About Orisha

Orisha is built on a simple idea: if you know something at compile time, compute it at compile time.

The Stack

  • Koru - Event-based language that compiles to Zig
  • Zig - Systems programming with comptime superpowers
  • Orisha - Web framework that exploits both

Philosophy

Traditional web frameworks do routing at runtime. They read files at runtime. They compute content-lengths at runtime.

Orisha does all of this at compile time. Your routes ARE your binary.
